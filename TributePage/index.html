Harshad Mehta

The Big Bull of Indian Stock Market

About Him

Harshad Mehta was an Indian stockbroker and businessman who rose to fame during the early 1990s due to his massive influence on the Indian stock market.

Why He is Remembered

He is remembered for revolutionizing the way people viewed the stock market in India. His life story highlights both the heights of success and the importance of ethics in finance.
