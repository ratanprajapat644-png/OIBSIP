Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India | People's President

About Him

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a crucial role in India’s missile and space programs and inspired millions of youth.

Why I Admire Him

I admire Dr. Kalam for his simplicity, dedication towards the nation, and his ability to motivate students to dream big and work hard.

Key Facts

  • Born: 15 October 1931
  • Known as: Missile Man of India
  • President of India: 2002 – 2007
  • Profession: Aerospace Scientist

Famous Quote
